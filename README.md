This project implements a **Management Strategy Evaluation (MSE)** to test the robustness of the fixed TAC rule (145,000 t/year) currently applied by **CCAMLR** for Antarctic krill (*Euphausia superba*) in Subarea 48.1 of the Western Antarctic Peninsula (WAP).

Two parallel MSE approaches are developed:

- **openMSE / MSEtool** — closed-loop simulation with a 9-scenario OM grid (sigmaR × climate shift) and two HCRs, conditioned on SS3 outputs.
- **FLR framework** — exploratory projections and short-cut MSE structure via `ss3om`, `FLasher`, and `FLfse`.

---

## Scientific Context

The current CCAMLR harvest control rule applies a fixed TAC of 145,000 t/year derived from the **γ = 0.1** precautionary escapement rule. This MSE evaluates whether that rule is robust to:

- Recruitment variability (`sigmaR` = 0.8, 1.2, 1.5) with AR1 autocorrelation
- Climate-driven reductions in stock productivity (R0 shift: 0%, −20%, −40%)
- Observation and process error in SSB and *F* estimation

**Reference period:** 1991–2018 (28 historical years). **Projection horizon:** 30 years.

**CCAMLR performance thresholds used:**
- *Conservation limit:* SSB/B0 > 0.20 (collapse reference)
- *Target:* SSB/B0 > 0.75 (CCAMLR escapement objective)

---

## HCRs Evaluated

| HCR | Description |
|-----|-------------|
| `CC145` | Fixed constant catch = 145,000 t/year (current CCAMLR rule) |
| `HCR_proportional` | TAC = 5% × B_obs, where B_obs = relative index × B0 |

## Workflow (`MSE_OpenMSE.R`)

```
Step 1  Read SS3 (r4ss) → historical SSB, recruitment deviations, Find
Step 2  Build biological arrays [nsim × 9 ages × 58 years]
Step 3  Simulate AR1 log-normal recruitment deviations (projection)
Step 4  Assemble Perr_y [48 × 66]: pre-history | historical | projection
Step 5  Extract historical fishing mortality (F:_1…F:_5 fleet columns)
Step 6  Build base OM (testOM template + cpars overwrite)
Step 7  Build 9-OM grid (sigmaR × R0 shift)
Step 8  Define MPs: CC145 and HCR_proportional
Step 9  Run MSE: 9 OMs × 2 MPs → mse_list
Step 10 Compute performance metrics (P_no_collapse, P_ccamlr_target, mean catch)
Step 11 Generate and save figures to fig/
```
